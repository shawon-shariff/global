WHAT IS

Global Entry allows pre-screened,low risk traveler to expedite the line ar security,immigration and customs using desgnated Global entry kiosk. This is not a program you can join instantly! Every applicant must fill out a lenthy Global Entry application foem - even child applicants must have their own Global Entry application submitted. When Customs and Border Protection receive the application they send it to a variety of US government agencies specifically, US law enforcement and US immigration as well as cheacking their own database for previous violations. Being classed as a 'low risk traveler' means that you have not committed any feleries that violate the Global Entry program.

The Global Entry Program benefits can be used by U.S. citizens, U.S. lawfual permanent residents & citizens of certain other countries.
To start the Global Entry process you must file an application & undergo for background and security checks. When you're Conditionally Approved you have to attend a compulsory interview. If you get approved you can start using the kiosks.
The Global Entry kisks can be used with a passport book or machine-readable lawful permanent resident card.

This stringent checking means that the United States can keep our borders safe!

The KTN PASSID number signifies tp security staff that you can expedite security at participating U.S. airports using the TSA lanes.

When you're a member of TSA PreCheck you must fill out your PASSID/KTN (Known Traveler Number) into the 'known traveler Number' section.

You must also book your flightuseing the exact same namethat you used when you signed up for the Global Entry program.

At the airport, check in for your flight andgo straight to the Precheck security lane.( Some airports don't have TSA Precheck lanes, which means you have no choice but to use the regular security.)
